ðŸ“š Lifecycle Hooks Explanation

Constructor: Called when component is instantiated
ngOnChanges: Called when input properties change
ngOnInit: Called after component is initialized
ngAfterContentInit: Called after content projection is initialized
ngAfterContentChecked: Called after content projection is checked
ngAfterViewInit: Called after view is initialized
ngAfterViewChecked: Called after view is checked
ngOnDestroy: Called when component is destroyed
